About the Author

Hadiza Bagudu

A gifted storyteller acclaimed for her diverse literary works that captivate hearts and minds. With a degree in International Studies from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, and as a Microsoft Certified Professional, Hadiza's passion for weaving compelling narratives shines through her writings.

Her stories explore the complexities of human relationships, the clash between tradition and modernity, and the enduring power of love to heal even the deepest wounds.
